This is a modern portfolio design, that is adaptable to any artist with a need to showcase their work. We start with a home page that introduces our artist. We also use Ajax and jQuery to allow the viewer to use the arrows to access the rest of the content.

Next the viewer learns a bit about the artist via a short blurb, or even a list of skills.

In this case we display a video project from the artist, but we can adapt to any artist's needs with the use of tiling, custom slider, etc. to showcase multiple pieces of content.

Lastly implemented custom email submition within the contact form, so that the viewer may get in touch with our artist.
